About Manadhal Inaivom

What We are Trying...

Manadhal Inaivom - Trust is making an important contribution towards strengthening the education, Improving Health & Hygiene, Facilitating Rural Development, Helping to Orphange and Ensuring Women Empowerments. We are committed to helping peoples of all the ages and abilities experience a more fulfilling life physically, spiritually, mentally and emotinally.

WHAT WE DO?

Education

A Good Education is extreamely essential for everyone to grow and succeed in life especially in rural area. Education increases self-confidence and assists in developing the personality of a person. Education plays a big role in our life. So we are focusing the governement school kids to develop their education, communication, encourage them into sports and individual talents. Also taking care of who all are no parents, we are taking care of their entire education life.

Read More

Welfare

Ageing cannot be prevented, but we can learn how to deal with arising conditions for great health of our loved ones. We are arranging Eye check up camps in our rural area, Blood donation, periodic health check up suggestions. Also conducting awareness programe about how to maintain our home, street, village in a cleanly manner.

Read More

Rural Development

Rural development plays an important role in every country. So we are focusing on peoples day to day life needs like water, cleanliness and development of Education, Higher education consultance, Sports, Public health and Sanitation, Infrastructure development, Facilities for agriculture extension, Employment opportunities, Women empowerment, etc.

Read More

Old Age Home-Orphanage

We have been helping to Old age homes, Orphanage Homes and Physically challenged kids Home for their day to day life needs like dress, foods. In a periodic manner, we are visiting their homes and providing what they need at that time. We are so happy to do this service.
