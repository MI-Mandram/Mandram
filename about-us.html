About Us

Strengthening Education, Improving Health & Hygiene to Senior citizens, Facilitating Rural Development, Helping to Orphanage and Ensuring Women Empowerments.

Manadhal Inaivom - Trust founded by Mr.S.Vetrivel, Mr.P.Karunakaran. Mr.S.Venkatachalam and Mr.M.Raja established on 2018. Manadhal Inaivom Trust is a Tamil nadu based NGO(Non Government Organization) on community development and capacity building within the local migrant and low - socioeconomic community. Working towards helping to sudents to achieve their goal and people to meet their dreams. We are as an organization believe that people should not be denied their rights because of where they were born.

We are committed to helping people of all the ages and abilities experience a more fulfilling life physically, spiritually, mentally and emotionally.

Our Focus On

    Education Enhancement Programs :


  • Distribution of educational materials such as note books, computers, school bags and infrastructure like furnitures and Projectors etc.
  • Rendering special coaching classes to all the students.
  • Concentrate an communication development in english
  • Organizing training to teachers on Teaching methodologies.
  • Imparting two days Soft Skill training and practically explain the science.
  • Provision of drinking water supply.
  • Provision of clean rest rooms for the students.
  • Planning for educational tours to the students.
  • Improving the infrastructure in public libraries and schools.

    Improving Hygiene and Health Programs to Senior citizens :


  • Support for the conduct of Health Programs, free medical camps, Immunization programs etc in Rural area.
  • Improvement of infrastructure in Health institutions particularly in rural needy institutions.
  • Support to the institutions dealing with mentally challenged children.
  • Support of infrastructure in Anganwadis, drinking water sources etc. for a hygienic environment.
  • Distribution of spectacles and performing surgeries to the needy senior citizens.

    Creating basic infrastructure through Rural Development activities :


  • Provide 24X7 Water facilities to our village.
  • To be kept the drainage Clean.
  • Provide the Street Lights.
  • Tree Plantation.
  • Build a Children's and Senior citizens Park.
  • Build a public toilets in our village.
  • Provision of drinking water supply.

    Women Development activities :


  • Imparting training programs on Computer, Tailoring etc. to create self employment to women entrepreneurs.
  • Distribution of revolving fund for supporting the business activities of Self Help Group members.
